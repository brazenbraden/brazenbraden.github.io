JuggleBee

JuggleBee is a Namibian online auction and marketplace platform developed using Ruby on Rails and aims to provide Namibians with a one-stop place to buy and sell items. It was launched in February 2014 and has grown steadily. November 2015 saw a complete re-write and overhall of the site bringing with it much needed capabilities and making it truely mobile friendly. Users can sign up for free via Facebook or manually and instantly start listing their items for sale or auction.

JuggleBee is my largest active project which I spend weekends and free time maintaining and adding new features.
